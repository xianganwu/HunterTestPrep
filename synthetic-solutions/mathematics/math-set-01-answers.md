## Detailed Solutions

### Question 1
**Answer: (C) 3/2**

2/3 + 5/6 = 4/6 + 5/6 = 9/6 = 3/2

Convert to common denominator (6), add numerators, simplify.

---

### Question 2
**Answer: (A) 1.7**

4.25 × 0.4 = 1.70

Multiply 425 × 4 = 1700, then place 3 decimal places: 1.700 = 1.7

---

### Question 3
**Answer: (C) $34**

15% of $40 = 0.15 × 40 = $6
Sale price = $40 - $6 = $34

---

### Question 4
**Answer: (C) 0**

(-8) + 5 - (-3) = -8 + 5 + 3 = -3 + 3 = 0

Subtracting a negative is adding a positive.

---

### Question 5
**Answer: (C) 21**

3 + 4 × 5 - 2 = 3 + 20 - 2 = 21

Follow order of operations: multiplication before addition/subtraction.

---

### Question 6

### Question 7

### Question 8

### Question 12
**Answer: (D) 4/5**
