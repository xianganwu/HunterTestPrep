## Instructions

This section tests your ability to analyze and compare mathematical quantities, recognize patterns, and apply logical reasoning.

- **(A)** if the quantity in Column A is greater

- **(D)** if the relationship cannot be determined from the information given

## Word Problems Requiring Reasoning

For questions 19-25, solve each problem using mathematical reasoning.

---

### Question 19

If all Bloops are Razzies and all Razzies are Lazzies, which statement must be true?

(A) All Lazzies are Bloops
(B) All Razzies are Bloops
(C) All Bloops are Lazzies
(D) Some Lazzies are Razzies
(E) No Bloops are Lazzies

---

### Question 20

Five friends ran a race. Maya finished before Tyler but after Sam. Tyler finished before Kira. Dana finished last. Who finished second?

(A) Sam
(B) Maya
(C) Tyler
(D) Kira
(E) Dana

---

### Question 21

### Question 24
