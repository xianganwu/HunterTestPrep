# HunterTestPrep

A comprehensive resource for Hunter College Campus Schools admission test preparation in New York City.

## Overview

This repository contains sample questions, test format information, and preparation resources for:

- **Hunter College High School (HCHS)** - 7th grade entry
- **Hunter College Elementary School (HCES)** - Kindergarten entry

## Test Information

### Hunter College High School (HCHS)

The HCHS entrance exam is offered to eligible 6th graders residing in New York City. The exam consists of:

| Section | Questions | Time (suggested) |
|---------|-----------|------------------|
| Reading Comprehension | 50 multiple-choice | 70 minutes |
| Writing Assignment | 1 essay (up to 2 pages) | 40 minutes |
| Quantitative Reasoning | 37 multiple-choice | 35 minutes |
| Mathematics Achievement | 47 multiple-choice | 40 minutes |

**Total Time:** 185 minutes (approximately 3 hours)

### Hunter College Elementary School (HCES)

HCES uses standardized cognitive assessments for Kindergarten entry. The school strongly discourages test preparation as it can invalidate results.

## Getting Started

1. Review the [Test Format Guide](docs/test-format.md)
2. Practice with sample questions in the `questions/` directory
3. Check your answers against solutions in the `solutions/` directory
4. Review [Test-Taking Strategies](docs/strategies.md)

## Important Notes

- **No calculators allowed** on the HCHS exam
- **No guessing penalty** - answer every question
- Official sample materials are available at [hunterschools.org](https://www.hunterschools.org/high-school/admissions/entrance-exam-2026)
